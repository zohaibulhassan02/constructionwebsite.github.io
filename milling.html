Milling

Home / Milling

Advanced Paving and Masonry's milling services stand out for their remarkable attention to detail. Their milling process begins with a thorough inspection of existing pavement or concrete surfaces and a careful plan of action. The work then involves precision demolition, using specialized equipment to remove the surface material efficiently and effectively. Following that, they use their high-powered milling machines to groom and shape the surfaces with great precision. Their milling services are special because they ensure that patched segments are blended in seamlessly, creating a uniform surface that lasts for years. The attention to detail throughout the process is unmatched, leading to perfectly finished and long-lasting results time after time.



With Advanced Paving and Masonry, you can trust that your milling services will be carried out with the utmost care, skill and attentiveness. Their commitment to excellence ensures a job done right!
